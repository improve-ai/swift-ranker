  • optimize(_:)

    Generates all combinations of the variants from the variantMap, and chooses the best one. For example, optimize([“fontSize”:[12, 13], “color”:[“#ffffff”, “#000000”], “width”: 100]) would first generate a list of variants like this: [ [“fontSize”: 12, “color”:“#ffffff”, “width”: 100], [“fontSize”: 12, “color”:“#000000”, “width”: 100], [“fontSize”: 13, “color”:“#ffffff”, “width”: 100], [“fontSize”: 13, “color”:“#000000”, “width”: 100] ] and then choose one of them.
